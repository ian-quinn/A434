Laboratory

Testing and Evaluation on Efficiency Technologies of HVAC System Energy

The facility is used for performance evaluation on energy efficiency technologies of HVAC system. It can also be used to test different control strategies, including CAV control, VAV control, fan frequency conversion control, air distribution control, exhaust air control,etc.

Testing and Evaluation on Efficiency Technologies of Windows and Constructions

The facility is used to conduct test on different windows, measure heat conduction coefficiencies and solar heat gain factor of windows, validate overall energy efficiency performance of windows.
