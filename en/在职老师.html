Yan Zhou
Vice director of Institue for Advanced Study of Tongji University
She has more than 16 years of experience in green building design consulting and low carbon planning. Research on the sociological impact of sustainable technologies, low-carbon cities, green building technologies and sustainable energy technologies in large public hubs。
  •   School of Mechanical Engeneering,Tongji University CaoAn Road 4800
Education & Work experience
2010.6~2011.10 Universite Paris-Dauphine Master degree； 1993.9~1997.6 Zhejiang University Bachelor degree；LEED Fellow；WELL AP； Green building youth council Committee member.
Green building technology
Since 2006, she has taken charge of more than ten green building designs as green building consultant, there were LEED, BREEAM, Green building certification of China and so on, some of this works were awarded. She is the first LEED Fellow in China，and she as Chinese representative took part in USGBC round-table conference several times.
Healthy building technology
PM2.5 indoor control, Work Place, Healthy building standard and research, Healthy building design and so on.
Building performance of operation
Such as the "Technical Guidelines for Green Buildings in Large and Complicated Spaces", which is aimed at the sustainable technology research in spacious spaces such as airports. The Shanghai Municipal Commission for the Construction of the Railways "Shanghai Green Star Building (Office and Exhibition) Operations Effective Research”, research on the operation of the green building projects that have been operated to provide reference for the revision of the green building standards; GIZ “Jiangsu Province Green Building Post-Operation Training Project Phase I”, government capacity building project; World Sustainable Industry and Commerce Board of Directors (WBCSD) Building Energy Efficiency Laboratory (EEB 2.0) China Project Leader.
Low carbon city
"Hangzhou Qianjiang New City Sustainable Development Technology Research" is a study on the sustainable technology of the completed CBD area and the mega-building, "Technical Guidelines for Low Carbon Ecological Building System in Damuwan Bay, Xiangshan, Ningbo", regional low carbon technology research, it involves residential, office and hotel building research projects, involving sustainable low-carbon technology measures for urban and single buildings; Ali Foundation “Qingyuan Action Old City Laundry Sewage Disposal Research Public Welfare Project”, and research on rainwater pollution.
Energy planning
ADB Ningbo's capacity to strengthen low-carbon development (Phase II) Project (Regional Smart Grid Research) Foshan New Energy Planning Project and other research topics, related to demand, micro-grid, energy station and other related technologies. UNEP, Regional Energy Experts Group.



Jianguo Shi
Research field:Industrial ventilation
  •   School of Mechanical Engeneering,Tongji University CaoAn Road 4800
Education & Work experience
1988~1995，Major in HVAC, Master degree and bachelor study at Tongji University. After his master degree study, he joined a famous construction company in Shanghai, his main job was project management, and there were several big projects on his charge。Since 2002，he focused on energy saving and efficiency of HVAC system, his work includes heat pump, radiant ceiling, micro-pipe heat exchange and novel dehumidification on air conditioning. Meanwhile, he has done some researches on IoT and equipment monitoring, these works were very success. Now, his work is about energy saving in the agriculture field, including renewable energy and clean energy use in real applications
