Asian Building Operation Data Consortium

Asian Building Operation Data Consortium Data Sharing Agreement

This Agreement (“Agreement”) is intended for institutions and individual researchers interested in contributing and gaining access to building operation data hosted by Asian Building Operation Data Consortium (ABODC). The data base contains building energy and performance data contributed by ABODC members.

1. Background

Building operation data is essential for research in the field of building science and simulation. Universities and institutions in Asia desire access to detailed building operation data in order to conduct certain scientific research and building data analyses. ABODC is willing to provide access to such data in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

2. Definitions

The following terms shall have the following respective definitions:

2.1 “Affiliate” means any university, research institution, and non-profit organizations, corporation or noncorporate entity, and individual researchers.

2.2 “Data” means the specific building related information, building design drawings, descriptions, specification, energy audit report, energy performance data, building automation system data, and data of all kind and in any form relating to a particle building.

2.3 “Research Study” means that specific noncommercial research study prepared and to be performed by affiliate.

2.4 “Researchers” means the specific individuals identified by Institution and Universities, who has requisite qualifications and experience to perform the Research Study.

3. Data Sharing

3.1 Research Purpose and Scope. ABODC shall provide access to the Data to Institution, and Institution shall access and use the Data, solely in order for Institution to perform the Research Study in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement, and for no other purpose. Institution shall not transfer the data to third parties and shall not access or use the Data for any commercial purposes.

3.2 Return or Destruction of Data. Upon completion of the Research Study and all associated obligations, Institution shall return or destroy, all of Confidential Information, including the Data. Institutions have no right to hand the data any third parties without written approval from ABODC.

3.3 No Representations or Warranties or Liabilities / Indemnities on Data Suitability. Each member affiliate and ABODC provide the Data an “as is” basis and disclaims all express and implied warranties of any kind, including any warranties of quality, accuracy, timeliness, completeness of the Data, and of merchantability, non-infringement and fitness for a particular purpose. To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, ABODC hereby excludes all liability for and Institution agrees to indemnify, defend and hold harmless ABODC from and against any claim, action, proceedings, damages or payments suffered by Institution that may arise as a result of the Institution’s use of Data provided to Institution by ABODC.

3.4 Performance of Research Study. Institution shall perform, and shall have the Researchers work to perform the Research Study with the highest standards of diligence and in accordance with the generally accepted scientific principles, good analysis practices, and all applicable laws, rules and regulations, including without limitation ethics committee review, patient data privacy and informed consent requirements.

4. Confidentiality.

4.1 Confidential Information. Institution shall hold in confidence of building identity and its owner’s information relating to its business, operations and products, including but not limited to, Data, any technical information, additional details of the Research Study provided to third Party Researchers, know-how, trade secrets, all sensitive, strategic information and method descriptions that it discloses to the Institution in connection with this Agreement.

4.2 Use and Disclosure. Institution represents and warrants that it shall use the Confidential Information only for the Purpose. Institution represents and warrants that it shall not use the Confidential Information for any other purpose.

4.3 Standard of Care. Institution shall protect the Confidential Information using not less than the same care with which it treats its own confidential information, but at all times using at least reasonable care.

5. Publication of Results

5.1 Institution agrees to submit the results of the Research Study for publication in the peer reviewed literature or at a scientific congress (a "Publication") in a timely and complete manner, with such Publication appropriately disclosing the strengths and weaknesses of the Research Study methodology. Institution shall submit to ABODC an advance copy of any proposed Publication at least two weeks days before submission to a scientific congress or journal for the purpose of reviewing the summaries and Publication for confidential or proprietary commercial information. Upon ABODC’s request, all such confidential or proprietary commercial information shall be removed from the summaries and publication.

6. Term and Termination

6.1 Term. The term of this Agreement shall commence as of the Effective Date and continue until all obligations under the Agreement have been fulfilled, unless terminated earlier upon notice by ABODC at any time.

6.2 Survival. Termination or expiration of this Agreement shall not relieve either Party of any obligation or liability accrued prior to the termination date. The obligations of the Parties under the Sections entitled: Confidentiality, Property Rights, Publication of Results, Survival, Assignment, and Miscellaneous shall survive termination of this Agreement.
