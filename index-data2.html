About

Building operation data is essential for research in the field of building science and simulation. Universities and institutions in Asia desire access to detailed building operation data in order to conduct certain scientific research and building data analyses. Asian Building Operation Data Consortium (ABODC) is a non-profit organization established to gather and share building operatoin data for research purpose. The data base is composed of building energy and performance data contributed by ABODC members. It is only open to ABODC members. If you want to become a ABODC member, qualified data contribution is needed. We will publish all contributed data on this website with permission.

For contribution requirements, please contact us at email: xupeng@tongji.edu.cn.
